About NorCal EVs

NorCal EVs is the Bay Area's premier alliance of electric vehicle clubs, uniting enthusiasts and owners from across Northern California’s diverse EV community.


From Teslas and Rivians to Ford F-150 Lightnings, Mustang Mach-Es, and Lucid Air sedans, our members share a passion for electric mobility and sustainable transportation.


Through our meetups, showcase events, and group drives, we create opportunities for both experienced EV drivers and curious newcomers to connect and learn from each other.


Our alliance serves as a unified voice for the Bay Area’s EV community and accelerates the transition to an all-electric future.
