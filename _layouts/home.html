About

NorCal EVs is a registered 501(c)(7) community built around real-world connection. That means monthly meetups, group drives on good roads, show & tells, and casual education events — Tesla, Rivian, Ford, Lucid, Hyundai, Kia, BMW, and everyone still deciding. Membership is simple, the first event is always free, and there's no test you have to pass to belong.
