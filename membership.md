## Become a Member

Welcome to NorCal EVs! We're excited to have you join our growing community of electric vehicle enthusiasts across Northern California.

### How to Join

Joining NorCal EVs is easy and free! Simply:

1. **Join our Heylo community** - [https://heylo.group/norcalevs](https://heylo.group/norcalevs)
2. **Follow us on social media** - Stay connected on Facebook, Instagram, and X
3. **Attend an event** - Check out our upcoming meetups and drives

### Membership Benefits

- Access to exclusive EV meetups and drives
- Connect with fellow EV owners across all brands
- Stay informed about the latest EV news and technology
- Participate in group purchases and discounts
- Join educational workshops and tech talks
- Be part of Northern California's EV revolution

### All Brands Welcome

Whether you drive a Tesla, Rivian, Ford Lightning, Lucid, Hyundai, Kia, or any other electric vehicle - you're welcome here! Our community celebrates all EVs and brings together enthusiasts from every corner of the electric vehicle world.

### Get Started Today

Ready to join? Head over to our [Heylo community](https://heylo.group/norcalevs) and introduce yourself!
